How GlobalBridgeOne handles information, protects your privacy, and complies with applicable law — explained clearly.

Overview

Who We Are and What This Policy Covers

This Privacy Policy applies to the website globalbridgeone.com, operated by GlobalBridgeOne, a technology company specialising in mission-critical AV, security, and command infrastructure solutions.

This policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, how it is used, and what rights you have in relation to it. We take your privacy seriously and handle any data we receive with care, transparency, and in accordance with applicable law.

In plain terms: this website currently collects minimal data. We do not run advertising, we do not sell or share your information with third parties, and we will always tell you clearly what we hold and why.
Data Collection

What Information We Collect

Currently, this website does not collect personal data through automated means. There are no sign-up forms, user accounts, cookies tracking your behaviour, or analytics tools running on this site at this time.

The only personal information we may receive is information you choose to send us directly — for example, your name, email address, phone number, or project details — when you contact us via email, WhatsApp, or any enquiry form that may be present on the site.

This information is used solely to respond to your enquiry and to discuss a potential professional engagement. It is not added to marketing lists or shared with any third party.

Future Changes

Analytics and Cookies

We may in the future add website analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or a privacy-first alternative) to help us understand how visitors use the site and improve the user experience. If and when this occurs, this Privacy Policy will be updated to reflect it, and where legally required, we will obtain your consent before any tracking begins.

At the time of writing, no analytics, tracking scripts, or third-party cookies are active on this website.

Data Use

How We Use Your Information

Any personal information you provide to us through direct contact is used only for the following purposes:

Responding to enquiries — to reply to questions, requests for proposals, or project consultations you initiate.
Project communication — to manage and coordinate ongoing professional engagements where you are a client or partner.
Legal and compliance obligations — where required by applicable law or regulation.

We do not use your information for marketing, profiling, or automated decision-making. We do not sell, rent, or otherwise transfer personal data to third parties.

Retention

How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was provided — typically the duration of a project enquiry or engagement, plus a reasonable period afterwards for follow-up or legal purposes.

If you request that we delete your information and there is no overriding legal obligation to retain it, we will do so promptly. See the section below on your rights for details on how to make such a request.

Your Rights

Rights Under Israeli and EU Law

Depending on your location and applicable legislation, you may have some or all of the following rights in relation to personal data we hold about you:

Right of access — to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
Right to rectification — to ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
Right to erasure — to request deletion of your personal data, where no legal ground exists for continued retention.
Right to object — to object to the processing of your data in certain circumstances.
Right to data portability — to receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format where technically feasible (GDPR, Art. 20).

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us directly using the details at the bottom of this page. We will respond within 30 days.

Israeli Privacy Protection Law, 5741–1981 GDPR (EU) 2016/679 Israeli Privacy Regulations (Data Security), 5777–2017
Third Parties

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes. We do not share your details with any third-party marketing platforms or data brokers.

In the limited case where an external service provider assists us in operating the website or conducting our business (for example, a hosting provider), such parties are contractually obligated to keep your information confidential and may only use it for the specific purpose authorised.

Privacy Contact

Questions, Requests & Complaints

If you have any questions about this policy, wish to exercise your rights, or have a concern about how your data has been handled, please contact us directly. We take all privacy enquiries seriously and will respond within 30 calendar days.
