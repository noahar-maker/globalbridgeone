Accessibility
Statement

GlobalBridgeOne is committed to making its digital presence accessible to all users, in accordance with applicable standards and regulations.

Compliance Status

Our Accessibility Commitment

Partial Conformance — WCAG 2.1 Level AA

GlobalBridgeOne is actively working to ensure that globalbridgeone.com meets the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA, as published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C).

This site also strives to comply with the Israeli Equal Rights for Persons with Disabilities Regulations (Accessibility Adjustments to an Internet Service), 5771–2011, and their subsequent amendments. We recognize that accessibility is an ongoing effort and are committed to continuous improvement.

Standards & Framework

Applicable Guidelines

Our accessibility work is guided by the following frameworks:

WCAG 2.1 Level AA
Web Content Accessibility Guidelines published by the W3C, covering perceivability, operability, understandability, and robustness of web content.
Israeli Accessibility Regulations (5771–2011)
The Equal Rights for Persons with Disabilities Regulations governing internet service accessibility in Israel, as amended and updated.
What We've Implemented

Accessibility Features on This Site

The following accessibility measures are currently in place across globalbridgeone.com:

Colour Contrast
Text and interactive elements maintain a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1 against their backgrounds, meeting the WCAG 2.1 AA minimum requirement for normal text.
Alternative Text for Images
All meaningful images include descriptive alt attributes. Decorative images are marked with empty alt text so screen readers skip them appropriately.
Keyboard Navigation
All interactive elements — navigation links, buttons, and contact actions — are fully operable using a keyboard alone. A visible focus indicator is provided throughout.
Semantic HTML Structure
Pages use proper heading hierarchy, landmark regions (nav, main, footer), and ARIA labels to provide a clear document structure for assistive technologies.
Skip Navigation Link
A "Skip to main content" link is available at the top of every page, allowing keyboard and screen reader users to bypass repetitive navigation.
Responsive Design
The site is fully responsive and usable across a range of devices and screen sizes, including mobile phones and tablets, without loss of content or functionality.
Known Limitations

Areas Under Improvement

While we strive for full WCAG 2.1 AA conformance, some areas of the site are still being reviewed and improved:

Certain third-party content embedded from external sources (such as fonts or image services) may not fully conform to our accessibility standards. We are working to audit and address these elements on an ongoing basis.

We conduct periodic internal reviews to identify and remediate accessibility issues. If you encounter any barrier not listed here, please contact us directly — your feedback directly informs our improvement roadmap.
